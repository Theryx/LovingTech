PRD: Loving Tech – Minimalist Catalog v1.0Project Lead: Ndouken TheryxObjective: A high-conversion, simple storefront for Loving Tech to sell premium gadgets (Logitech, Anker, Keychron) in the Cameroon market without the friction of a full checkout system.1. Project OverviewA minimalist e-commerce web application that acts as a digital catalog. Instead of a payment gateway, it captures leads via a simple form and funnels users directly to WhatsApp to finalize the sale.2. Target AudienceTech professionals and fintech workers in Cameroon.Users who value authenticity (avoiding "counterfeit" tech).Customers who prefer "Pay on Delivery" or "MoMo" transactions.3. User FlowBrowse: User views a clean grid of products on the home page.Filter: User filters by brand (Logitech, Anker, Keychron).Detail: User clicks a product to see technical specs and "In the Box" details.Inquiry: User clicks "Order via WhatsApp."Lead Form: A modal appears asking for WhatsApp Number and Delivery Address.Redirect: Data is saved to Supabase, and the user is sent to WhatsApp with a pre-filled message.4. Core FeaturesA. Homepage & CatalogGrid Layout: Clean, high-res product cards.Brand Tabs: Simple navigation for specific brands.Search Bar: Quick search by product name.B. Product Detail Page (PDP)Spec Sheet: Technical details (e.g., DPI for mice, Wattage for chargers).Trust Section: * "100% Authentic" badge.Delivery timelines (24h Douala / 48h Yaoundé).Gallery: Simple image slider to show different angles.C. The "No-Checkout" FormFields: WhatsApp Phone Number, Neighborhood/Address.Action: Submit button triggers a database entry and a wa.me redirect.5. Technical RequirementsThe StackFrontend: Angular (to practice your current learning) or React/Next.js.Styling: Tailwind CSS (Minimalist/Dark Mode theme).Database: Supabase (PostgreSQL) to store product data and lead entries.Deployment: Vercel.Database Schema (Supabase)Table: productsTable: leadsid (UUID)id (UUID)name (Text)product_id (FK)price_xaf (Int)customer_phone (Text)specs (JSONB)delivery_address (Text)image_urls (Text[])status (Text: New, Contacted, Sold)6. Trust & Localization (Cameroon Focus)Currency: Prices explicitly in XAF.Payment Mention: Clear text stating "Pay on Delivery / Mobile Money accepted."Community Link: A floating button linking to your existing WhatsApp community.
